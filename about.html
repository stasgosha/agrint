Agrint was established in 2016 order to detect un-visible trees pests and reduce the massive spraying of pesticides around the world by focusing treatments on infested trees only.
The company, founded in 2016 by army veterans, has a background and experience in the field of sensors and communications in the military industry.

After years of development, Agrint created the IoTree technology. This innovative solution is based on a durable, energy-efficient sensor that is highly sensitive to the slightest movement of the larvae at their early stages of life, and thus provides the time-critical early detection of their activity, regardless of their size or the size of the tree. The company adapted its sensor to detect the Red Palm Weevil larvae - an insect causing catastrophic damage to palm trees worldwide.

Since then, the company became a global pioneer in the field of early detection of larvae in trees. The first targeted market where the palm trees and its 4 billion trees worldwide. The 4 main types are: Dates, Oil, Coconut and Ornamental palms. Agrint also detects other pests like the Oryctes, Fig/mango stem borer, Asian long horn beetle and many more. In 2020 Agrint released its next generation of products, the “IoTrap”, a comprehensive management system for agriculture that enables farmers to track fruit fly populations in real-time.

We develop unique, sustainable solutions that positively impact the planet’s environment, economy and humanity.

  • Highly sophisticated algorithms-based on state of the art seismic sensors.
  • Large scale deployments made possible through Agrint’s IoT BLE mesh network.
  • Big data analysis provides world coverage, scalability and reliability with fast processing of local and regional data for identifying proliferation trends & improving at a specific alert level.
  • Agrint’s solution does not require any special regulation or expertise to operate.
