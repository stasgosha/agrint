Setting Up IoTree® App on Your Phone

App store/google store

Searching Iotree App

install

Download

  • Open the Google Play Store (Android) or App store (IOS) on your phone.
  • Search for “ IoTree® “ on the store and tap on IoTree® as it comes up in the search results.
  • Tap install to download the app for your phone
  • Tap accept to allow IoTree® app access to your location.
  • To launch the application, tap on the application icon once the “ IoTree® “ app has downloaded on your phone.
  • When you first launching the app, you must register with the Username + Password received from Agrint’s support team, enter your username and password.
  • If this is the first sign in, you may need to change your credentials, and supply the system with a recovery method for your account, such as an additional email or mobile phone number. You are required to provide a one-time-password in order to proceed.
  • When the registration is complete, the customer selection page will be displayed.
  • Select the customer you want to view the data by clicking on the customer’s name, then the main screen will be displayed.
